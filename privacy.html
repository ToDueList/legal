Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

ToDueList ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by ToDueList.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a personalized pregnancy task tracking experience.

  • Account Information: When you use the app, we utilize Anonymous Authentication via Google Firebase to create a secure, unique User ID for you. We do not require your name or password.
  • Pregnancy Data: We collect the Due Date you provide to calculate your personalized task timeline.
  • Usage Data: We track which tasks you mark as complete to visualize your progress.
  • Purchase History: We use RevenueCat to process and track your subscription status. We do not store your credit card information directly; all financial transactions are handled securely by Apple's App Store.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your anonymous account.
  • To calculate your pregnancy timeline and serve relevant weekly tasks.
  • To restore your purchases and manage your subscription access across devices.

3. Third-Party Services

We share data with the following trusted third-party providers solely to function:

  • Google Firebase: For secure user authentication and database storage.
  • RevenueCat: For managing in-app subscriptions and purchase validation.
  • Apple App Store: For processing payments.

4. Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and data at any time. To request deletion, please contact us at theToDueList@gmail.com or use the "Delete Account" option within the app settings.
